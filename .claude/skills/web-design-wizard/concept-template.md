# Concept Template

Use this template when creating `concept.md` in Phase 5. **Only include sections you have data for** — omit anything that would be placeholder text.

```markdown
# [ProjectName] - Business Concept

**Created:** [Date]
**Content Language:** [English/Danish/etc.]

---

## Business Overview

**Type:** [Landing page/Portfolio/Blog/E-commerce/SaaS/etc.]

**Purpose:** [1-2 sentence description]

**Target Audience:** [From discovery]

**Value Proposition:** [If known from discovery]

---

## Brand & Voice

**Mood/Feeling:** [From discovery]

**Tone of Voice:** [Inferred from mood — formal, casual, technical, friendly]

**Key Messages:**
- [Inferred from page type and audience]

---

## Design Direction

**Desired Aesthetics:**
- [From discovery preferences]

**Anti-Patterns (What to Avoid):**
- [From discovery anti-preferences]

---

## Content Strategy

**Primary Content Sections:**
1. [Section 1 - e.g., Hero/Introduction]
2. [Section 2 - e.g., Features/Benefits]
3. [Section 3 - e.g., Call to Action]

---

*This concept document guides all design and implementation decisions.*
```

## Guidelines

- Populate from discovery phase responses ONLY
- Do NOT fabricate competitors, metrics, or technical requirements the user didn't mention
- Keep it concise — this is a reference for sub-agents, not a business plan
- If discovery was brief, the concept.md should be brief too
